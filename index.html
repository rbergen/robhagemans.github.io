A range of hardware and GW-BASIC dialects

PC-BASIC interprets GW-BASIC/BASICA and its IBM PCjr and Tandy 1000 extensions. Full sound capabilities and a range of emulated video modes are supported, including classic CGA, Hercules and Olivetti modes.
