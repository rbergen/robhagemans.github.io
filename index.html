Get PC-BASIC now!

Where you need it, when you need it

PC-BASIC is 99.9% compatible with Microsoft® GW-BASIC™ and runs on all modern desktop operating systems. An Android app is available for all your mobile BASIC needs.

Whether you're running Windows XP or above, Mac OS X 10.6+ or your favourite Linux distribution, PC-BASIC brings the line numbers you need to the device that you choose to use.

Powerful yet elegant

PC-BASIC incorporates state-of-the-1980s technology such as subroutines, event handling and computed GOTOs in an easy-to-use, procedure-oriented architecture that appeals to amateurs and professionals alike.

Leverage the power of a highly familiar environment to short-circuit that all-important time to market for your applications.

Colourful and musical

PC-BASIC supports colour graphics modes up to 640x350 EGA and monochrome resolutions as high as 720x348 and 640x400. Choose up to 16 attributes out of an amazing 64 colours!

The IBM® PC™ speaker's extensive sound generation capabilities are a great match for the powerful Music Macro Language that can be accessed through the PLAY statement.
